Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from RupeeLetter App, you can request account deletion using the method below.

How to request account deletion

  • Send an email to: contact@rupeeletter.com
  • Use subject: Delete My Account
  • Mention your registered email ID.

What data will be deleted?

  • User account information
  • Saved preferences
  • Associated app data

Data retention

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days. Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for legal or security purposes.
